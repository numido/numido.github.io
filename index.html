Use case

Synfrizz study
Full report
Radiolabeled OTSA101, a monoclonal antibody targeting synovial sarcoma (SS) developed by OncoTherapy Science, was used to treat relapsing SS metastases following a theranostic procedure: in case of significant 111In-OTSA101 tumor uptake and favorable biodistribution obtained on SPECT images, patient was randomly treated with 370/1110 MBq 90Y-OTSA101.
In that study, we provide comprehensive Monte Carlo 3D dosimetry from time sequence of SPECT images. Time-activity curves extracted from 111In-OTSA101 repeated SPECT/CT in various organs and tumors were integrated. Estimated absorbed doses (AD) in normal tissues were compared to biological side effects and to the admitted maximal tolerated absorbed dose (MTD) in normal organs. Results in the tumors were also compared to disease evolution.
